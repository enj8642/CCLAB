The Idea

The Couch

With dreams of hitting the jackpot, Alex decided to go to the glittering world of a local casino. Finding a few dollars from the back of the couch, Alex set out to the casino.

The Casino

Entering the casino, the dazzling lights and the hypnotic sounds of slot machines welcomed Alex into a world of possibilities. With heart pounding, Alex approached the BLACKJACK table, with a mixture of excitement and nervousness. He had never played BLACKJACK before, so the dealer explained the rules:

Goal: The Goal of BlackJack is to get a value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.

Card Values: Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value. Face cards are each worth 10. Aces can be worth 1 or 11, whichever is better for the hand.

How To Play: You and the dealer are dealt two cards each. You can choose to "hit" (get another card) or "stand" (keep your current hand). If your total exceeds 21, you lose (bust). If you don't bust, the dealer hits until their hand is 17 or higher. If the dealer busts or if your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's, you win.

Winning conditions: If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without busting, you win. If the dealer busts, you win.

The Realization

Luck was on Alex's side! Just from a couple of games in BLACKJACK, Alex's money skyrocketed. Inspired by this initial success, Alex decided to try his hand at the slot machines. However, the hard earned money that he gained from BLACKJACK seemed to all have gone.

Rules for Slots:

Money/Betting: Insert coins or bills into the slot machine, and decide how much money you want to bet per spin.

Spin/Wait for Outcome: After spinning the slot machine, the reels will spin and then stop, revealing a combination of symbols.

Winning: If the symbols on the reels match a winning combination, you win the bet.

The Loss

In a final act of desperation, Alex placed a the rest of his money on a high-stakes roulette game. Alex decided to hedge his bets on a color... Luck had abandoned Alex, and the final bet failed. With empty pockets and a heavy heart, Alex left the casino, realizing that sometimes, making money fast just isn't possible.

Rules of Roulette

Place Your Bet: Place your chips on the roulette table to bet on where you think the ball will land.

Wheel Spin: A small ball is dropped onto a spinning wheel (roulette) in the opposite direction.

Win Condition: The ball settles into one of the numbered and colored pockets on the wheel. If the ball lands in a pocket that matches your bet, you win!
